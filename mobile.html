How to play Cards Against Top Brick?

One of you is chosen as the Card Tsar.

The black square is the QUESTION CARD, and the white ones are the ANSWER CARDS.

To begin, everyone presses the SHUFFLE button to generate new question and answer cards.

The Card Tsar then reads his QUESTION (Black) card out loud. Everyone must then answer with their funniest/weirdest/etc ANSWER (White) card, including the Tsar.

After everyone answered, the Card Tsar awards ONE POINT to the funniest answer. The person who gave that answer then gets one point.

And then, a new Card Tsar is chosen, and the game continues! Big woop.

p.s. keep track of your points by using the Your Points buttons next to the Shuffle button! Don't cheat though!
